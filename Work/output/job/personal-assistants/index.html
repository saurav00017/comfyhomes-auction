  • Full Time
  • On-site; main office location
  • Posted 2 weeks ago

$20-25/hr+

Salary: $20-25/hr+

Location: On-site at various locations

Job Description:
– Seeking highly organized Personal Assistants to provide comprehensive support to executives
– Responsibilities include managing schedules, coordinating meetings, booking travel arrangements, and handling confidential information
– Candidates must have exceptional time-management and multitasking abilities, along with strong communication skills
– This role requires a proactive approach and the ability to adapt to changing priorities
– Reports to assigned executive or manager

Requirements:
– Bachelor’s degree preferred, but not required
– At least 2 years of experience in a similar role
– Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite and calendar management tools
– Ability to handle sensitive information with discretion

Benefits:
– Competitive hourly wage
– Full-time role with benefits, including health insurance and retirement plans
– Opportunities for career growth within the organization
– Positive and professional work environment
